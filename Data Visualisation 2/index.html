The Connection of GDP and Population to The World

GDP stands for the Gross Domestic Product which measures the total economic output of a country, reflecting its economic health and performance. GDP per capita calculates the average income of each individual in a country and is an indicator of living standards. The total population of a country reflects its size and demographic composition.

GDP By Country
